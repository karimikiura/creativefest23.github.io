About Creative Festival 2023

Over the past few years, the leading record companies namely Warner Music Group, Sony Music, and Universal Music group have entered the African market. This signifies the demand for African content in the international market and the need for a collaborative approach to unlock the potential of the sector. The creative festival brings together artists from Kenya, Nigeria, South Africa, Ghana, and the USA for a week-long festival. It seeks to unlock the potential of the sector through collaboration-creation and commercialization of talent

Creative Economy hackathon

22nd -23rd of February 2023, at Close the Gap, Mombasa County. It seeks to bring together aspiring Musicians, Film Makers, And Fashion artists to think through ideas in the sector and provide solutions that can be commercialized. Trainings on digital skills

The choral Festival

To be held on 24th and 25th February 2023 at Swahilipot Auditorium. The focus of the festival is to Showcase great choral music. Empowering artists with the insights, tools, and resources needed to be able to turn talent into a business.
